> Invoicing, cash register, bank accounts, credit cards, chart of accounts, and payment tracking

| **Dependencies** | `clients` |

## Description

The financial plugin handles the complete accounting lifecycle: invoices (payable and receivable), cash register sessions, bank account management, credit card transaction tracking, chart of accounts, cost centers, and payment method configuration.

**Value beyond CRUD:** This is a multi-view dashboard plugin with 6+ sub-pages, not a single entity CRUD. It manages complex financial workflows: invoice creation with line items and discounts, cash register open/close sessions, installment tracking, bank statement reconciliation, and payment deletion audit trails. It also injects a "Payments" tab into `clients.detail.tabs`.

## Custom Pages

### Financial Dashboard (`/financial`)

Multi-view dashboard with sidebar navigation:

| View | Route | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Overview | `/financial` | Revenue/expense charts, KPIs, period comparison |
| Receivables | `/financial/receivables` | Invoices to receive (from clients) |
| Payables | `/financial/payables` | Bills to pay (to suppliers) |
| Cash Register | `/financial/cash-register` | Open/close sessions, current balance |
| Credit Cards | `/financial/cards` | Card transaction tracking, fee configs |
| Bank Statements | `/financial/statements` | Bank account statements and reconciliation |

### Invoice Detail Page

Line-item invoice with:
- Multiple payment methods per invoice
- Discount and adjustment tracking
- Installment scheduling
- Attachment support (DANFE, receipts)
- Status: `draft` → `sent` → `partial` → `paid` → `overdue` → `cancelled`

### RPC Functions
- `sync_invoice_payment_status(uuid)` — Recalculate invoice status based on movements
- `validate_cash_register_status()` — Ensure only one open session per account
- `link_invoice_item_to_journey()` — Trigger: link invoice to sales journey

### Storage Buckets

## Key Workflows

### Invoice Creation (from appointment)
1. Appointment completed → financial plugin auto-creates receivable invoice
2. Line items populated from appointment_services
3. Staff selects payment method(s)
4. Financial movements created (one per payment)
5. Invoice status updated based on total paid vs. total due

### Cash Register Session
1. Staff opens cash register → records opening balance
2. All cash transactions during the day are tracked
3. At end of day, staff closes register → counts cash
4. System shows expected vs. actual balance with difference

### Bill Payment
1. Admin creates payable invoice (supplier bill)
2. Sets due date and payment method
3. On payment date, records the financial movement
4. Invoice moves to `paid` status
